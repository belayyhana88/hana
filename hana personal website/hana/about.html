About Me

Hi, I'm Hana Belay Seyum, an ambitious professional with a strong background in computer science and a growing passion for global business and economics. Originally from Ethiopia, I was raised in a small but loving family by my incredible single mother, who instilled in me the values of resilience, hard work, and continuous learning.

I earned my bachelor’s degree in computer science from one of Ethiopia’s top universities, where I developed a deep understanding of technology, software development, and problem-solving. After graduating, I worked with multiple companies, gaining hands-on experience in the tech industry. These professional experiences not only strengthened my technical expertise but also exposed me to the real-world challenges and opportunities in the field.

However, I always had a desire to expand my career and knowledge beyond technology. I realized that understanding the business world, its structure, strategy, and global impact could help me bridge the gap between IT and business innovation. With this goal in mind, I moved to Italy to pursue a master’s degree in Global Economy and Business. This opportunity has given me international exposure, a broader economic perspective, and the ability to connect technology with business solutions on a global scale.

My ultimate goal is to become a leading professional at the intersection of technology and business. I aim to leverage my expertise in both fields to create innovative solutions, drive digital transformation, and help businesses make data-driven and technology-driven decisions.
